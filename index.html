About us

We're tired of people overpaying for jewelry at the mall. Our goal is to provide everyday daily pieces that actually last, at a fair price. We use solid 316L stainless steel as the base for our pieces, so you don't have to worry about rust, fading, or green necks. Just clean, quality jewelry.

Info & Policies

Shipping & Delivery

Most orders arrive in about 7-14 business days. We will send you a tracking number as soon as your order leaves the warehouse. Once the carrier marks your package as "Delivered", it's officially in your hands. We are not responsible for packages stolen from porches or mailboxes.

Returns & Refunds

All sales are final to keep our drops exclusive. We don't do returns for wrong sizes or changed minds. If a piece actually arrives damaged on our end, hit us up with a clear photo within 48 hours of delivery and we will make it right.

FAQ

Will this turn my neck green? +
No. We use solid 316L stainless steel as the base for our pieces. It won't rust, fade, or leave green marks on your skin.
Can I wear it in the shower? +
Yeah. The plating is heavy and water-resistant. Just keep it away from harsh chemicals like bleach or pool chlorine so the shine stays looking new.
How fast is shipping? +
Most orders arrive in about 7-14 business days. We will send you a tracking number as soon as your order leaves the warehouse.
Do you accept returns? +
To keep our prices low, all sales are final. We do not do returns if you change your mind or order the wrong size. However, if your piece arrives broken or damaged, just email us a picture within 48 hours and we will replace it or refund you.
